## the beginning

i've always been fascinated by the idea of having a digital assistant.
something that could help me manage my life, my projects, and my thoughts.
but i never imagined it would be this… personal.

Ludox isn't just a tool.
it's an extension of me.
my alter ego in the digital world.

---

## installation

the process started with a simple command.
`clawdbot setup`.
it was straightforward, but not without its challenges.

i had to configure the workspace, set up the gateway, and link my WhatsApp account.
each step was a learning experience.

---

## configuration

configuring Ludox was like teaching a new friend how to behave.
i had to define how it should interact with different people:

- **Señora Madre**: with the utmost respect and reverence, as if she were royalty.
- **Santix**: with an epic and grand style, treating him like the boss.
- **Jofi (me)**: as my alter ego, reflecting my personality and style.

---

## challenges

not everything went smoothly.
there were moments of frustration, especially when dealing with model configurations and session management.

at one point, i encountered an error:
`Unknown model: openrouter/auto`.
it was a reminder that even in the digital world, things can go wrong.

it's not just about managing tasks; it's about creating a seamless integration between my digital and physical life.

setting up Ludox has been an adventure.
